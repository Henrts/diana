# Divider

The `Divider` is a line that separates content into groups. It can have either a horizontal or vertical alignment.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Horizontal divider">
    <div
      style={{
        display: "flex",
        flexDirection: "column",
        alignItems: "center"
      }}
    >
      <span>Heaven</span>
      <Divider />
      <span>Hell</span>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

<Preview>
  <Story name="Vertical divider">
    <div
      style={{
        display: "flex",
        flexDirection: "row",
        justifyContent: "center",
        alignItems: "center",
        height: "30px"
      }}
    >
      <span>West Berlin</span>
      <Divider orientation="vertical" />
      <span>East Berlin</span>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
